The QRCode Linkulator application lets you move a site you are browsing from your computer to your phone. There are three simple steps to get setup:

  1. drag this "linkulate" bookmark to your browser's bookmark bar or tool bar:
    linkulate

    If you are on Internet Explorer add that link to your favorites by right-clicking on it.

  2. Get the ZXing's barcode scanner or other QR Code enabled barcode scanner installed on your phone.
  3. When you visit a site you want to move to your phone, click the "linkulate" button in your desktop/laptop browser and scan the image in the popup window!
That's it!
